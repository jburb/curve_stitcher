About "Mashrabiya"

Mashrabiya explores rosette-like radial patterns and ornamental lattice structure derived from stitched constructions.

A useful distinction in this project is that discovered rosettes are not always constrained by the nested inner-circle steps common in classical compass-and-ruler constructions. That means some picked regions differ from textbook rosette partitions, even when they are consistent with the stitched line network.

Even with that difference, these discovered Mashrabiya patterns are still designed to support tiling behavior through repeated radial and edge-compatible motifs.
